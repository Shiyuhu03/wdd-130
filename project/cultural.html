History

Jiuzhaigou County is magical and charming. Since Yin and Shang became the land of Di and Qiang, it has a history of more than 3,500 years through the Han, Three Kingdoms, Jin, Wei, Southern and Northern Dynasties, Sui, Tang, Song, Yuan, Ming, and Qing Dynasties, leaving countless rich cultural heritages. It has been passed down to this day. The Nanping Minor Tune is the most local traditional folk art in Northwest Sichuan. Jiuzhai people play it with their homemade pipa, plus the concerto of plates and bells, giving people a beautiful enjoyment.

The landscape of Jiuzhaigou is characterized by karst geological landforms in alpine alpine regions, with lakes, waterfalls and shoals as the main body, water as the soul, and geological background as the foundation. Its main body is located on the Motianling nappe, and the strata The structure is dominated by construction and the degree of metamorphism is low; the structure is dominated by the development of northwest-trending faults and folds. The impact of geological background on the landscape of Jiuzhaigou is mainly manifested in two aspects: First, various geological processes such as structure, karst, and glaciers control the formation of Jiuzhaigou Valley , which is the basis and framework for the formation of Jiuzhaigou’s landscape; secondly, it controls the formation of Jiuzhaigou’s water cycle, and further controls the distribution of travertine bodies in the scenic area, as well as the unique water quality.

Food

Folk dances are represented by the "Twelve Phases" and "Praise" dances of the Baima Tibetans. The main content is to sacrifice gods, pray for peace, and drive away ghosts and evil spirits. They wear various animal masks when dancing, and their dancing postures imitate the movements of animals. They are rough and vigorous. ,lively.

The food culture is unique, especially "Zamian", "Potato Ciba", "Buckwheat Noodle Mixing", and "Jade Wheat Mixing". It is eaten with sauerkraut homemade by Jiuzhaigou people as the main seasoning. It is exquisite in workmanship, rich in nutrition and delicious. In particular, "Buckwheat Noodle Stirring" not only has a good fragrance, but also can prevent and treat diabetes if eaten for a long time. Village culture is another beautiful landscape in Jiuzhaigou County. Dalu and Batun, a thousand-year-old village, have 600 Tibetan houses and Tibetan Buddhist temples built on the mountain. They are magnificent, exquisite in architectural skills, and contain profound Tibetan culture.
